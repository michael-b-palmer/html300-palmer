Cherry Facts

Cherries belong to the same fruit family as peaches, plums, apricots, and almonds.
There are more than 1,000 varieties of cherries, but fewer than 10 are produced commercially.
Cherry trees can take about 3 to 4 years after planting before they start bearing fruit.
